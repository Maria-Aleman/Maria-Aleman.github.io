Final DMA Project

For the final project, Maria wanted to make another attempt at an Blender environment as her medium of choice.

This narrative is a critique for the lack of care that the world has for their environment and climate change in general. For the model, Maria referenced Yosemite National Park as it being a good example of one of the state's many coniferous forests that are impacted by human caused wildfires every fire season. She humanizes the national park with the bottom environment being made up of the materials as people, flesh, meat, veins, and bones.
