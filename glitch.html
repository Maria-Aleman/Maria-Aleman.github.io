The Glitch Project

In this project, Maria tried out editing images through Notepad++ and Audacity to create a glitch result. The format of the images from left to right as it increases creates this sense of esclataion with the battle through eating disorders.

Little by little, the food I used to love don't look as appetizing as I remember. I'm not hungry anymore.

The more I look at myself in the mirror, I can't even recognize myself anymore. Who's this monster that's looking back at me?

I feel so full. I can't carry my weight around. I need to cleanse myself from all that nastyness from my body, I'll look cuter that way.
