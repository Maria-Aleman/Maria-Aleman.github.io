Fears to Fathom

Fears to Fathom presents grounded common fears (home invasion, creepy motels) in a episodic psychological horror, the first thing that comes to mind with these two games is atmosphere. Everything about it is quite claustraphobic and anxiety-inducing. The first game, "Home Alone" is free to download. Because it's episodic, there'll be a third game released around the fourth quater of the year, so keep your eyes out!
