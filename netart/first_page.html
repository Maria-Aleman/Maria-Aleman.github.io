My Beautiful Paper Smile


It's a psychological horror presented in a hand drawn sketchy look which adds to the creepy element. The premise revolves around escaping a facility where kids are the prisoners and are forced to wear permanent smiles via the masks. You're met with danger at every turn, from the guards trying to stop you and the beings that live in the shadows. It's still in production and currently has three chapters out.
