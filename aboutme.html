Let's Meet the Artist

Maria Aleman is a 22-year-old Mexican American illustrator who majors in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Coming from the small town of Hollister, California, she aspires to work in the animation industry as a art director. Maria originally started out as a traditional artist, however throughout the years she's grown to prefer digital art programs as her medium of choice; art programs she typically uses are Procreate and Clip Studio Paint.
